# STYLE FIDELITY — {{STYLE_NAME}}

Fidelity controls style freedom. It does not weaken the identity lock of an approved character.

## 100%

Closest approved anchors for every applicable role. Prefer deterministic editing for small changes. Change only explicitly released attributes.

## 90%

Complete visual language remains dominant. New outfit, pose, scene, and small personalization are allowed.

## 70%

The style is clearly dominant, but compatible references may be mixed and design freedom is broader.

## 50%

Balanced hybrid of the source style and a new art direction. Preserve several defining systems, not isolated motifs.

## 30%

Recognizable influence with broad design freedom. Existing-character face and body identity still remain locked at 100%.
